## Brand guidelines for every design

Keep all designs consistent so your feed looks professional and recognizable:

- **Colors:** green `#00c700` (accent), blue `#009ee6` (primary), dark navy `#1d2a39` (text/footer), white background.
- **Fonts:** Quicksand (headlines), Poppins (body). Canva substitutes: Quicksand and Poppins are both in Canva.
- **Logo:** place your wordmark or leaf symbol in a corner of every post.
- **Always include:** phone **(410) 231-3076** and **myhomecares.com** somewhere on the image.
- **Tagline:** "Where Service Matters."
- **Tone:** warm, trustworthy, calm. Real or warm stock photos of seniors and caregivers, never clinical or cold.
- **Sizes:** Instagram/Facebook feed 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1350 (portrait). Instagram Story/Reel cover 1080x1920. LinkedIn 1200x627. Nextdoor 1080x1080.
- **No em dashes** in any caption (brand style).
