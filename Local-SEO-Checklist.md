## 1. Google Business Profile (highest impact)

This is the listing that shows on Google Maps and the local "3-pack." It outranks almost everything else for searches like "home care near me."

**Claim and verify**

- [ ] Go to business.google.com and claim/create "My Home Cares" (sign in with your info@myhomecares.com Google Workspace account).
- [ ] Complete verification (Google will mail a postcard, or offer phone/video, follow the prompts).

**Fill it out completely (a 100% complete profile ranks better)**

- [ ] **Primary category:** Home Health Care Service. Add secondary categories: Home Care Service, Nursing Agency, Aged Care.
- [ ] **Service area:** add each county/region you serve (you don't show a street address if you work in clients' homes, set it as a service-area business).
- [ ] **Phone:** (410) 231-3076. **Website:** https://www.myhomecares.com
- [ ] **Hours:** set real hours; mark 24/7 availability if you take calls around the clock.
- [ ] **Description:** mention licensed Maryland RSA, your services, your regions, and your differentiators (OHCQ-compliant training, Medicaid waiver welcome, fast start).
- [ ] **Services:** add each one (Skilled Nursing, Personal Care, Companion Care, Respite Care, Homemaking, Meal Planning) plus specialized care (Alzheimer's & Dementia, Parkinson's, Post-Surgery, 24-Hour/Live-In).
- [ ] **Photos:** add 10+ real photos, your team/caregivers, logo, and a few warm in-home care images. Profiles with photos get far more clicks. Add new photos monthly.
- [ ] **Attributes:** check anything that applies (e.g. "Identifies as women-owned," "Online appointments," "Onsite services").

**Use it weekly (signals you're active)**

- [ ] Post a **Google Update** every 1-2 weeks (a tip, a service spotlight, a seasonal note). It takes 2 minutes and keeps the profile fresh.
- [ ] Turn on **messaging** and answer quickly.
- [ ] Seed the **Q&A** section with 3-5 questions families ask (cost, licensing, how fast care starts) and answer them yourself.

## 2. Reviews engine (your biggest trust lever)

Reviews drive both ranking and the decision to call. Aim for a steady trickle, not a one-time burst. The website is already built to display real reviews and show star ratings in search results, you just add them as you collect them.

**Set up the ask**

- [ ] In Google Business Profile, find your **"Ask for reviews" short link** (Home > Get more reviews > Share review form). It looks like `https://g.page/r/XXXX/review`.
- [ ] Send me that link, or paste it into the site config (`reviewUrl` in `src/lib/site.ts`) so every "Leave a review" button points straight to the form.

**Make asking a habit**

- [ ] After care is going well (about 1-2 weeks in), text or email the family a thank-you with the review link.
- [ ] Ask in person too, families are happy to help when prompted.
- [ ] Respond to **every** review, positive or negative, briefly and professionally. Responding is a ranking signal and shows you care.

**Sample request message (text or email):**

> Hi [Name], it's been a pleasure caring for [loved one]. If you have a moment, a short Google review would mean a lot to our caregivers and helps other Maryland families find us: [your review link]. Thank you! , The My Home Cares team

**Put real reviews on the website**

- [ ] As you collect Google reviews, send them to me (or add to `src/lib/testimonials.ts`). They'll appear on the homepage and `/reviews`, and enable star ratings in Google search results.

## 4. A few more high-leverage moves

- [ ] **Hospital & facility relationships:** discharge planners and social workers refer constantly. A simple one-pager (services, counties, "care can start in days," phone) left with local hospitals and rehab centers can drive real referrals.
- [ ] **Google Search Console (done):** check back weekly under Indexing > Pages and Performance to see what's getting indexed and which searches bring traffic.
- [ ] **Backlinks:** ask partners, associations, and any local press to link to myhomecares.com. Quality local links boost ranking.
- [ ] **Track your phone:** ask every new caller "how did you find us?" so you know what's working.

---

## Suggested rhythm

- **Weekly:** 1 Google post, respond to any new reviews, send 1-2 review requests.
- **Monthly:** add fresh photos to your profile, add any new reviews to the website, check Search Console.
- **Quarterly:** audit your top directory listings for accurate NAP, refresh your service descriptions.

Consistency beats intensity. A little every week compounds into strong local visibility over a few months, which is exactly the timeline that supports your goal of 100 clients by year-end.
